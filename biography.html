About Me

Education

After graduating from Bishop McGuinness Catholic High School in Oklahoma City, Oklahoma. I briefly attended Acadia University in Wolfville, Nova Scotia, where I studied music. I left Acadia before finishing my degree and came to Canada's capital, Ottawa. Here, I received a diploma in journalism from Algonquin College. I plan to graduate from the University of Ottawa in the spring of 2021.

Experience

I interned at The Guardian, Prince Edward Island's newspaper of record, for six weeks in the spring of 2016.
